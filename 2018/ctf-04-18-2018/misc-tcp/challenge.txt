Super Interesting Message

Your friend has a super secret server that contains super secret messages and you've figured out how it's protocol. Can you find potentially world-changing message within?

The Server will send 5 packets consisting of 2 4-byte integers, the first being an unsigned random number and the second being the numebr of the packet (numbered 1 through 5)

You should send back 5 packets consisting of 2 4-byte integers, the first being the sum of the digits of the first random number and and the second being the number of the packet

After sending the 5 packets you will receive a special message
